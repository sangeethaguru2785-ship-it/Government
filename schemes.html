Step by Step

How to Apply for Any Scheme

Follow these simple steps to apply for any government scheme on our platform.

1
Create Your Account

Sign up on Stackly using your Aadhaar number and mobile phone. Complete your profile with personal, income, and occupation details.

2
Check Eligibility

Use our smart eligibility checker to find all schemes you qualify for based on your profile information.

3
Choose a Scheme

Browse available schemes, read details, compare benefits, and select the one that best fits your needs.

4
Upload Documents

Submit required documents digitally — Aadhaar, income certificate, caste certificate, bank details, etc.

5
Submit & Track

Submit your application and track its status in real-time. Receive SMS and email updates at every stage.

6
Receive Benefits

Once approved, benefits are directly transferred to your bank account via DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer).

Documents You'll Need
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Passbook
  • Passport Size Photo
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Disability Certificate (if applicable)
