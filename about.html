About Us

Transforming Governance Through Technology

Stackly is a pioneering initiative by the Government to bridge the gap between citizens and public services through digital innovation. Our platform serves as a single point of access for all government services.

Since our inception in 2011, we have continuously evolved to meet the changing needs of citizens, leveraging cutting-edge technology to make government services more accessible, transparent, and efficient.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create a citizen-centric digital ecosystem that simplifies access to government services, ensures transparency in governance, and empowers every citizen with the tools they need for seamless interaction with public institutions.

Our Vision

To build a fully digital governance framework where every citizen can access any government service from anywhere, at any time, creating a paperless, queue-less, and stress-free experience for all.

Our Values

We are guided by principles of transparency, inclusivity, innovation, and accountability. Every feature we build is designed to put the citizen first and ensure equal access to government services for all.

What Drives Us
