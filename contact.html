FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us.

What is the average response time for emails?

We aim to respond to all email inquiries within 24 hours during working days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our toll-free helpline at 1800-180-1551 for immediate assistance.

Can I visit the office without an appointment?

Walk-in visits are welcome during working hours (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM). However, for complex queries, we recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure the concerned officer is available.

How do I track my complaint or grievance?

After filing a complaint, you will receive a tracking ID via SMS and email. You can use this ID on our portal's grievance tracking page or call our helpline to check the status of your complaint.

Is there a toll-free number for technical support?

Yes, our technical support helpline is available 24/7 at 1800-180-1551 (Toll Free). You can also reach us via email at support@stackly.gov.in for portal-related issues.

Can I get assistance in regional languages?

Yes, our helpline supports Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Kannada, and Gujarati. You can also visit regional offices for assistance in local languages.
