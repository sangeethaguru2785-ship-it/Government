General Questions

What is Stackly?

Stackly is a unified digital platform launched by the Government to provide citizens with online access to various government services. It allows you to apply for certificates, pay taxes, track applications, and access government schemes - all from one place.

How do I create an account on Stackly?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. You will need to provide your basic details including name, email, phone number, and Aadhaar number for verification. After verification, you can set your password and start using the services.

Is Stackly free to use?

Creating an account on Stackly is completely free. However, some services may have applicable fees as prescribed by the respective departments. The fee amount will be clearly mentioned before you proceed with any application.

Is my personal data secure on Stackly?

Yes, Stackly uses enterprise-grade security including 256-bit SSL encryption, multi-factor authentication, and strict data privacy protocols. Your personal information is stored securely and is never shared with unauthorized third parties.

Services & Applications

How long does it take to process an application?

Processing times vary by service. Simple certificates like income or caste certificates typically take 3-7 working days. More complex services like driving license or property registration may take 15-30 days. You can track the status of your application in real-time through your dashboard.

What documents do I need to upload?

Document requirements vary by service. Generally, you may need proof of identity (Aadhaar/PAN), proof of address, photographs, and service-specific documents. The exact list of required documents will be shown when you select a service to apply for.

Can I apply for multiple services at once?

Yes, you can apply for multiple services simultaneously through your citizen dashboard. Each application will be processed independently, and you can track all your applications from one place.

What if my application is rejected?

If your application is rejected, you will receive a detailed reason for rejection. You can rectify the issues and reapply, or file an appeal through the grievance redressal system. Our support team can also assist you in understanding the rejection reason.

Payment & Refunds

What payment methods are accepted?

Stackly accepts multiple payment methods including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit cards, credit cards, net banking, and digital wallets. All payments are processed through secure payment gateways.

Can I get a refund if my application is not processed?

Yes, if your application is rejected or cannot be processed, the applicable fee will be refunded to your original payment method within 7-10 working days. You can track refund status through your dashboard.

How can I track my refund status?

You can track your refund status by logging into your Stackly account, navigating to "My Applications" section, and checking the payment history. Refund details including the expected credit date will be displayed there.
